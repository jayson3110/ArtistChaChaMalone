Cha Cha Malone

Cha Cha Malone is a Seattle-based singer, composer, and music producer


A prodigious songwriter, he started to write songs on the piano as well as beats on his computer at the age of 11.


He gained widespread popularity and recognition through his partnership with his longtime friend Jay Park, founder of AOMG and H1GHR MUSIC, serving as the producer for the majority of the beats on Jay Park’s albums “New Breed” and “Take a Deeper Look”.


He further cemented himself as one of Korea's top producers with Jay Park’s 2016 hit album “Everything You Wanted", having produced 13 of the 19 tracks on the album.


Recently, from his new single ‘All Eyes On You (2020),’ Cha Cha participated not only as a producer, but also showing his new aspect as a vocalist, made him to gain a lot of positive respond from his fans


Cha Cha is known for his versatility and willingness to explore different and unfamiliar musical styles: He has worked with a wide range of artists including hip hop artists such as Dok2 and Beenzino, mainstream K-pop groups such as Red Velvet and Shinhwa, and R&B artists such as Brian Joo.


Cha Cha Malone은 시애틀에서 활동하고 있는 가수, 작곡가 및 음악 프로듀서이다.


그는 11살때살 때 컴퓨터로 비트를 만들고 피아노로 곡을 쓰기 시작하며 아티스트로서 성장해왔다.


이 후 Cha Cha Malone 은 AOMG와 H1GHR MUSIC의 대표 박재범과의 오랜 친구로서의 인연을 음악으로 보여주며 국내에서도 많은 인기와 리스너들의 인정을 받았다.


박재범의 정규앨범 [New Breed(2012)], [Take A Deeper Look(2011)]에 그의 곡이 발표되며 국내에 이름을 알렸으며, 많은 사랑을 받았던 [EVERYTHING YOU WANTED(2016)]에서 총 19개 트랙 중 13개의 트랙을 프로듀싱하며 최고의 프로듀서로 자리매김했다.
